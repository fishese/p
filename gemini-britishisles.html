The DIY City Detours

For the urban hubs, skip the crowded tour buses. You have the savvy to use their fast, modern transit systems. Grab a coffee, wander at your own pace, and soak in the city vibes.

Edinburgh

Newhaven Port

Transit: Newhaven Tram

5-min walk to stop. 25-min tap-to-pay ride to Princes Street.

  • • Walk the historic Royal Mile.
  • • Browse shops below the Castle.
  • • Try a local savory pastry.

Belfast

City Center

Transit: Taxi/Shuttle

The Independent Foodie Map

Skip the tourist menus. Here are the artisanal, highly-rated local spots perfect for two friends looking for authentic flavors and a great atmosphere.

The English Market (Cork)

A stunning municipal food market operating since 1788. Wander the stalls for artisan Irish cheeses, fresh seafood, and handmade chocolates. Grab lunch on the mezzanine at Farmgate Café.

Bread 41 (Dublin)

Located near Pearse Station, this is Dublin's premier independent bakery. Legendary for their long-fermentation organic sourdoughs and creative morning pastries.

The Winding Stair (Dublin)

A historic bookshop-turned-restaurant overlooking the River Liffey. It offers quintessential, unpretentious Irish home cooking in an incredibly cozy setting.
